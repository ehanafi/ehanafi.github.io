About Us

The Michigan Cat Club (MCC) is a student run organization at the University of Michigan - Ann Arbor. Focused around student mental health, the MCC brings students and cats together in hopes to spread awareness about our feline friends, helping student well-being with our emotional support kittys, and creating adoption oppertunities.
