Our Mission

The Michigan Cat Club (MCC) strives to create a better life for both students and animals. Our mission is the establish a community of cat lovers who are intested in helping to ensure good care and protection for pet companions, stop pet overpopulation, and encourage an adopt not shop mindset. To help students destress and feel love from an animal companion.
